We are Fleet MUCKY MAYHEM Cycling!

We are a local group of cyclists within the Fleet (Hampshire) and surrounding areas that enjoy exploring off-road routes along the local towpaths away from the traffic.

We welcome new and experienced riders, whether you have just bought a shiny new MTB/Gravel bike or dug your old bike out of the depths of the shed/garage and wiped off the cobwebs!

Coming Up: About Us...

About Us

Our regular (3 out of 4 Saturdays) off-road rides are 1.5-2.5 hours - Typically Minley camp area, Caesar's camp, and towpath to Mytchett canal cafe, all advertised in our WhatsApp group.

Once a month we also aim to offer a longer ride, please check out our Ride Diary.

Ride style is relaxed, easy-paced social riding at 8 - 10 mph average - We wait for everyone!

We welcome all age groups, although under 18 year old riders must bring along their own adult -lol

Ride Diary

Here is a list of our past and planned rides.

If you are interested in coming along, reach out to us through the Contact Us section.

Date Title Destination Miles Ride Time Guidance Notes
17/Feb/24 Lock, Stock & Barrel Bisley 27 3.5hrs Easy going along towpath, Cafe at NSC. 4 went, great breakfast. ...uphill on way back! -lol
09/Mar/24 War of the Worlds Woking 35 4.5hrs Easy going along towpath, H.G. Wells statue, Martian Alien Tripod sculpture, Cafe at The Lightbox Art Gallery.

5 went, the weather was good. The Cafe at the Lightbox was great!

We had one mechanical which was dealt with whilst at the cafe.
13/Apr/24 Rags to Riches Weybridge 48 6hrs

Easy going along towpath.

All options for shorter rides and trains from Brookwood (14m), Woking (18m), Weybridge (24m)

5 set out, we met one more on the way, 5 and half? :)

We had a comfort stop at Mytchett Canal cafe, then headed to Weybridge, we walked through the High St (traffic was heavy!) to Cafe Enzo, great menu and the food and coffee was good, reasonably priced! on the return, another stop at Mytchett canal cafe for tea and ice cream!

47 miles in total, approx 4hr 40mins ride time (6hrs 50mins total), 10mph average.
11/May/24 Burnt Cakes! Winchester 40 5-6hrs Towpath, Cross country, some tarmac. Return by train to Fleet.
08/Jun/24 City Slickers London 50 7-8hrs

Mainly towpath out to Weybridge, Wey navigation to Thames path, then into London.

Train back from Waterloo.

7 went. Towpath was firm going, stop for food and coffee at The Lightbox, Woking, arrived in 1 Hr 55 mins, approx 35-40 mins stop.

At Weybridge in 3Hrs 5 mins, averaging 11 mph to this point.

At Hampton Court in 4 Hrs 10 mins,the Thames path is generally in good condition and wide, there are occassional pinch points and can be lots of people, especially in areas with Rowing clubs by the river.

It felt as if we were in open countryside for the majority of the way.

Progress slowed in the last 4 miles as the route sometimes left the river side and also the volume of people increased.

We arrived at Waterloo after 7 Hrs 15 mins!

A great adventure for the day!
20/Jul/24 Alice in Wonderland Guildford 38 6hrs

Mixed riding including tarmac, a fun ride!

xx/Aug/24 DDOTTS Guildford to Shoreham-by-Sea 78 10hrs

DDOTTS - Daft Day Out To The SeaSide

Start at 08:30, Guildford Railway Station Main entrance.

Old disused railway line route, minimal tarmac.

Many options for shorter rides, cafe stop at 21 miles, thus folks can return from there.

Various options for trains back to Guildford. All can be catered for!

1. Ice Cream Run : Ride to Southwater Country Park Cafe - 21 miles each way (42 total)

2. Sails and Rails : Ride to Shorham by Sea, see the sails/boats and catch the train back, approx 38 miles

3. Full Monty : Guildford to Shoreham-by-sea and return ....76 miles!
