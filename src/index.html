We are Fleet MUCKY MAYHEM Cycling!

We are a local group of cyclists within the Fleet (Hampshire) and surrounding areas that enjoy exploring off-road routes along the local towpaths away from the traffic.

We welcome new and experienced riders, whether you have just bought a shiny new MTB/Gravel bike or dug your old bike out of the depths of the shed/garage and wiped off the cobwebs!
